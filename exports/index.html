How We Export

Seamless Export Process

Complete export documentation, professional packing, and reliable freight — we handle everything from quotation to delivery.

1. Quote Within 24h

Send us your drawing or requirements. We respond with a detailed quotation within 24 hours including unit price, lead time, and shipping estimate.

2. Order Confirmation

PI (Proforma Invoice) issued. Advance payment or LC accepted. Production begins upon payment confirmation.

3. Manufacturing & QC

Tools manufactured to your specified standard. Full dimensional inspection with report. Material Test Certificate (MTC) available.

4. Shipping & Documentation

Secure packing in wooden crates or cartons. Export docs: COO, packing list, commercial invoice, test certificate. DHL/FedEx or sea freight.
