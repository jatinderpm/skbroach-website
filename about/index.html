Who We Are

Precision Tool Manufacturer Trusted by Honda, Mahindra & HAL

SK Broach Enterprises was established in 2013 in Patiala, Punjab, India — a city with a long tradition of precision engineering and manufacturing. From our 30,000 sq. ft. facility, we manufacture High Speed Steel broaches, hob cutters, and ring gauges to the highest international standards.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company and an approved vendor for Honda Motorcycle & Scooter India, Maruti Suzuki, Mahindra & Mahindra, HAL (Hindustan Aeronautics Limited), and Ordnance Factory India. Our tools are manufactured to DIN (Germany), ASA/ANSI (USA), JIS (Japan), GOST (Russia), and BIS (India) standards.

We maintain a Germany liaison office in Offenbach to serve our European customers — providing local sales support, DIN standard expertise, and rapid response in CET time zone.

From Patiala to the World

Over a decade of growth — from domestic tooling supplier to international precision manufacturer.

Quality & Compliance

Certifications & Standards

Every broach we manufacture is dimensionally inspected before dispatch. Our quality system covers raw material certification, in-process inspection, and final dimensional verification.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System certification covering all aspects of design, manufacturing, inspection, and delivery.

DIN Standards (Germany)

DIN 6885 (keyway), DIN 5480 (spline), DIN 8002 (hobs) and full range of German industrial standards.

ASA / ANSI (USA)

ASA B17.1 (keyway), ASA B92.1 (involute spline) and full range of American National Standards.

JIS (Japan)

JIS B 1301 keyway standards and JIS spline standards for Japanese OEM supply chains.

GOST (Russia)

Russian GOST standard broaches for Eastern European and CIS market customers.

BIS (India)

Bureau of Indian Standards — for domestic Indian market and government procurement.
